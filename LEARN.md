## What You'll See

Demo mode loads a simulated Kenworth T909 with a Cummins X15 engine. All data is fake but realistic:

- **Engine** — RPM, coolant temp, oil pressure, turbo boost, exhaust temp, engine load, fuel rate, DEF level, DPF soot, regen status, fuel level
- **Drivetrain** — gear, trans temp, shaft RPMs, air pressures, diff temp, speed, odometer, battery, alternator
- **Faults** — sample active and inactive fault codes with SPN/FMI
- **ECU** — engine model, rated HP/torque, part numbers, software version, programmed parameters
- **GPS** — live coordinates, Google Maps link, speed, heading, ignition

Data refreshes every 5 seconds with slight variation to simulate a live truck.

## Share Reports

While in demo mode you can tap **SEND REPORT TO MECHANIC** on the Engine or ECU tabs. This generates a text report you can share via SMS, WhatsApp or email — or copy to clipboard.
